Places to Visit

Monkey Forest

Monkey forest is located in Ubud, Bali. It's a sanctuary for wild monkeys. You can visit them as they would be in their natural habitat, but watch out these monkeys might steal your belongings.

Image is from balidistinctiontours.

Kuta

Kuta is a town in Bali that has great night life as well as the beach is great for surfing. Be sure to visit this clearwater beach.

Places to Stay

Bali Bohemia

Bali Bohemia is located in Ubud. It has beautiful bungalows to stay in and it only $13.

Bulgari Resort Bali
