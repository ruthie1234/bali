Mount Angung

Image is from Geology

Mount Angung is a volcano located in the district of Karangasem in Bali. It is an active volcano and the highest point on the Island with an Elevation of 9944 feet. Its a challenging hike, but the done best at sunrise. The views of it is unbelievably beautiful.

Nusa Penida

Image is from GIRLEATWORLD

Nusa Penida is a little undeveloped island southeast of Bali in the district of Klunkung. It's known for it's natural beauty and Balinese people believed the island is full of black magic and that is where the dark spirits are kept.

WaterFalls & Springs

Image is from Hotels.com

In the deeper parts of Bali's jungle, hosts some of the most breathtaking waterfalls and springs with sightings of exotic birds and wildlife. Most of the waterfalls are located in Bali's central west.
